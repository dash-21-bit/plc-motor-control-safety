# I/O Mapping (Example)

## Inputs (Digital)
- I0.0 START_PB (NO)
- I0.1 STOP_PB  (NC)
- I0.2 ESTOP_OK (NC chain healthy = TRUE)
- I0.3 OVERLOAD_OK (NC chain healthy = TRUE)
- I0.4 RESET_PB (NO)

## Outputs (Digital)
- Q0.0 MOTOR_CONTACTOR
- Q0.1 ALARM_LAMP

## Internal bits
- M0.0 MOTOR_LATCH
- M0.1 FAULT_LATCH
